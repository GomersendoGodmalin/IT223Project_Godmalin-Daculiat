About Us

Phead.com started with two friends (Gomer and Dominic) with a shared love for headphones and the desire to share this passion with others. They strongly believe that great sound is one of life's purest pleasures and that quality audio devices by extension lead to a better life.

Our 3 business principles:

1. We seek out and source only the world's best audio equipment by evaluating products based on performance, quality and long-term user satisfaction. Our goal is to fulfill all your audio needs while ensuring that you enjoy your purchase for years to come.

2. Our team of agents consist of those who are always available to help support as well as product knowledge experts. Our aim is to provide service so good that you can't help but tell people about it.
